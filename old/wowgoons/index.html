How to join!

First and foremost, you need to select your desired membership level-

Bronze Membership

<Goon Squad>'s most basic membership. Along with a guild invite, BRONZE members get access to <Goon Squad>'s vast pool of crafters, Ventrilo server, and forums. BRONZE members also may do only the Heart of Fear, Mogu'shan Vaults, Terrace of Endless Spring, and Throne of Thunder Mists of Pandria 10 man instances, but are capped at saving no more than 250 DKP at any given time. Tier tokens are only purchasable with a premium bonus of just $4.95.

Silver Membership

Along with all the benefits of BRONZE membership, SILVER members are able to raid with <Goon Squad>'s 5.0 and 5.2 patch 25 man raids. SILVER members have their maximum DKP limit raised to 500. Upgrading to this membership level also allows members to gain Tier 14 and Tier 15 tokens and participate in <Goon Squad> pre-made honor farming battleground groups. SILVER members also are granted protection from 'Timeless Guile Goons', an elite task force of guild members equipped with Censure of Eternal Agony sworn only to crush any player character on the new 5.4 Timeless Isle.

Gold Membership

As well as all the previously listed membership benefits, GOLD members are given priority for Tier 14 and 15 tokens. The DKP limit is once again raised to 1000 total DKP, and the DKP cost of items is discounted 10%. The Mists of Pandaria 5.4 patch raid Siege of Orgrimmar is available for GOLD members as well. GOLD members are allowed access to officer chat and special GOLD-only forums. GOLD members also get raiding priority over lower memberships, and complete free access to all of the consumables in the guild bank. GOLD members may also choose to utilize the services of an authentic Chinese player to grow, maintain, and till crops for the Sunsong Ranch daily quest for a monthly fee of $7.95. (This feature requires you to NOT POSSESS AN AUTHENTICATOR.)

Platinum Membership

<Goon Squad>'s second highest membership level. PLATINUM members of course are awarded the bonuses of all the lesser memberships, but have no DKP limit, a 25% discount on the cost of DKP items, and a guaranteed slot in all raids. PLATINUM members are given exclusive access to experience progression raiding in 25 man Heroic instances. PLATINUM members are given moderator status on the forums, and the ability to mute lower members in guild chat and Ventrilo. Only PLATINUM members are considered for application to <Elitist Jerks>, an intense <Goon Squad> sister guild which otherwise is closed to new members.
